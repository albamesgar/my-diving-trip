Welcome!

In this web you will be able to mantain a register of all the dives you have done. You can see them in the Dive Book section, as well as add new dives to your dive book.

You will also have acces to your Diving Passport, in which you can store all your diving titulations.

In the Clubs section you can see the address and the contact information of the clubs existing in our data base. If you want to include a club or suggest a modification of the existing information, don't hesitate to contact us and we will try update our data base.

Finally, you also have a User section where you can check your user information and modify it.
